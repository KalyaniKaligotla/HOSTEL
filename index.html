Accommodation
  • KLEF (Deemed to be University) has separate hostels for girls and boys with well furnished rooms and modern amenities. The overall atmosphere is very conducive  for the students to concentrate on studies and research.
  • State-of-the-art kitchen and spacious dining area have been provided in all the hostels.
  • Emphasis has been laid on hygiene and cleanliness for healthy living. A customized menu caters to the student needs, and it keeps changing according to their tastes.
  • Wi-Fi enabled hostels.
  • Power backup through use of Generators.
  • Round-the-clock security and medical facilities are also available.
Girls Hostel

There are 3 hostels for girls. The girl’s hostels are within the campus. Different rooms accommodating 1 per room, 2 per room, 3 per room with attached toilets as well as AC rooms are also available. Deluxe rooms with modern furniture and separate study room are also available.

Facilities in the Hostels
  • Protected drinking water.
  • State of the art kitchen and dining hall are available.
  • Well maintained Rooms and toilets.
  • Basic amenities such as a cot, study table, chair, and a rack.
  • Each room is also well ventilated.
  • Gas & Steam based hygienic food preparation.
  • Palatable regional, national, and international cuisines
  • Safety and Security
  • Dispensary with part time medical officer and 24/7 Ambulance facility
  • Newspapers
  • Laundry
  • Stationary shop
  • Foreign nationals/NRI/PIOs feel at home in hostels.
Advantages of being an inmate of university hostel
  • Clean and green environment
  • Responsibility and accountability of each student
  • Safety and Security
  • Medical facilities
  • Library is easily accessible and is available till 09:00 PM
  • Convenient for students who are attending placement training sessions.
  • Coaching for competitive exams
  • Monitored Study Hours
  • Special classes for weak students

  • Very convenient for students who are engaged in extension activities.
  • Handy for people who are in hobby clubs.

  • Well-balanced diet under the direction of a nutritionist.
